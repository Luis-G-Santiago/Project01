Slow is smooth, smooth is fast.

Hello! I am third generation Master in the style of Kung Fu San Soo. I have been doing San Soo for over 12 years, and am based out of Simi Valley, CA. Kung Fu San Soo is a combat style art that is not to be compared with other sport styles. If you are interested in learning practical self defense, or Tai Chi, then please contact me to begin your journey. - Master Luis Santiago

Schedule of Classes

Kung Fu San Soo, Adults

Mon - Thurs, 7:00 PM - 8:00 PM

Kung Fu San Soo, Adults, is for adults 18 year old and over. Classes are Monday through Thursday, from 7:00 PM - 8:00 PM. Signed waiver forms are required before you may begin. Memberships start at $80/month or $10/class.

Kung Fu San Soo, Kids

Mon - Thurs, 6:00 PM - 7:00 PM

Kung Fu San Soo, kids, is for kids between 6-14 years old. Classes are Monday through Thursday, from 6:00 PM - 7:00 PM. Signed waiver forms are required before you may begin. Memberships start at $80/month or $10/class.

Tai Chi

Fri, 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM

Tai Chi, is for adults 18 years old and over. Classes are only Fridays, from 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM. Signed waiver forms are required before you may begin. Memberships start at $40/month or $20/class.
